Accounts:

Why create an account?

Your account will be used to save all your progress in the cloud so you can retrieve it on any device.This account will also be used to display your name on the class leaderboard. When setting up your account, you do not need to use an actual email adress as it will be used for authentication only and not to contact you. Your name can be whatever you want and it will be the name displayed on the class leaderboard.

Create an account:

  1. Click on the 'mon compte' button at the top of the screen. Then click 'Cr√©er un nouveau compte'.

  2. Enter a chosen name into the 'nom' input, enter an email into the 'E-mail' input and enter your password into the 'mot de passe' input. You will then need to type your password again into the last field. Click the button to finish.

Sign In

  1. Click on the 'mon compte' button at the top of the screen. Then click 'Se connecter'.

  2. Enter your email into the 'E-mail' input and enter your password into the 'mot de passe' input. Click the button to finish. You should now be logged in and all your progress will be saved to your account.

Privacy:

Your account data is not shared with anyone outside of this website. On signing up, it will collect a nickname, an email address and a password. Your score achieved on the built in games is then stored in the database and used to create an automatic leaderboard. The leaderboard will display your nickname and score, not your email or password, which will be visible to anyone using this site. If you do not want any of this visible, do not sign in and your score will be kept in a localStorage cookie only, without a name. localStorage cookies are not cleared.

Updates:

This website recieves frequent updates, which bring new features,styling upgrades, bug fixes and performance imporvements. However,in most browsers this website is often stored in cache, meaning it is saved locally to your device to save it having to be completely loaded everytime. This can sometimes mean you don't recieve updates. Press this button to hard reload this website and clear all cache, to make sure you have the latest updates. This will not clear any of your data on this website.
